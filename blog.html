Calligraphy and Lettering have been a passion of mine for the past few years and really drive a lot of the activities I find myself doing in my "spare time." (as if there is such a thing)
Some of those activities include...

  • A 365 Project
  • Starting (two others): Cards for Dudes
  • Starting online communities or accounts on Instagram such as LetterBattle or Calligrafight
  • Another small business in the works...

Yes, this is a lot. Yes, I am aware. I am in the process of minimalizing my projects so I can really focus on the ones that will have the most impact. :)
